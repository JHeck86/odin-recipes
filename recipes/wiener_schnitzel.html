Wiener Schnitzel

Description:

'Schnitzel' means basically meat in a crust. I'm German and hope you can understand my English description. Serve the schnitzels with salad, ketchup and French fries.

Ingredients:

  • veal
  • eggs
  • bread crumbs
  • oil
  • flour
  • salt&pepper

How to:

  1. Cut the veal into steaks, about as thick as your finger. Dredge in flour. In a shallow dish, beat the eggs with 1 tablespoon oil, salt and pepper. Coat the veal with egg mixture, then with bread crumbs.
  2. Heat a quarter cup oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat. Fry veal until golden brown, about 5 minutes on each side./li>
